Hey there! I'm James.

 

I'm an atmospheric chemistry graduate student at the University of Washington, a UX/UI enthusiast, and a digital humanist with a special interest in Asian American history.

 

Some of my passions include digital accessibility, thick mapping, computational text analysis, UX research, remote sensing, and science education. I'm interested in the intersections of air quality, statistics, and history, particularly in how human-environment interactions affect people's sense of place.
