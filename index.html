Hey there! I'm James.

I'm an atmospheric chemistry graduate student at the University of Washington, a UX/UI researcher, and a digital humanist with a special interest in Asian American history.  

My scientific interests are in remote sensing, air pollution, and the atmospheric oxidative capacity. Outside of science, I'm fascinated by human-environment interactions, expressed through art, artifacts, and oral histories.
